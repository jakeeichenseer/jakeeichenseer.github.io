#1 Central Coast

In the fifth publication of Shredding California, San Luis Obispo again ranks number one on the central coast for mountain bike destinations. Slocals attribute the win to San Luis Obispo's diverse terrain and mix of technical features with flowy singletrack. Between Madonna, West Cuesta, Irish Hills, Pismo Preserve, and MDO, it's really no surprise to see SLO back on top.

Climbing Everest

See how this slocal MTB legend climbed the height of Mount Everest by riding to the top of Madonna 39 times. Dubbed Project Impossible-Everest, it took over 24 hours.

Employees Only

The Central Coast Concerned Mountain Bikers lobbies for unrestricted access to MTB trails that fall within the Camp San Luis Boundary, such as Undercover Buddy, Employees Only, and Pick and Shovel.

Paying for Pismo

Riders will now have to pay for access to Pismo Preserve, the beloved trail system in Pismo Beach known for its pristine singletrack and iconic SLO FLow.

Race Ready

Sign up for the ninth annual Central Coast Classic, where riders from all over the central coast will be competing in the region's most memorable downhill sprint to the finish.

Slocal Deals

Visit Foothill Cyclery on Foothill Blvd. for deals on all your favorite MTB products. From tires to helmets to goggles and everything in between, they've got you covered.

Content Creation

Check out Slocal legend Sean Haran's newest mountain biking edit on YouTube, featuring cinematic shots of your favorite West Cuesta Ridge trails, such as Firebreak, Chrome, and TV Tower Road.

East Cuesta

Lesser known unsanctioned trails on East Cuesta Ridge such as Pinecone and Bulldozer have recently been stirring up concern in the MTB community. Riders speak out about wanting official access.

Trail Damage

MTB tire tracks were recently reported on the Felsman Loop in Ferini Ranch Open Space, a nature preserve where mountain biking is not permitted. Hikers were especially frustrated due to the trail damage.

Rainy Conditions

A storm is expected to hit SLO on Sunday, May 29. Please stay off the trails until conditions are clear and the trails are dry so as not to damage them for other riders.

Cal Poly Cycling

The Cal Poly Cycling Team is offering free bike repairs and maintenance from 12 to 4pm on Dexter Lawn this Friday. Just show up with your bike and student ID and you're good to go.

Summer in SLO

Summer definitely creates some challenges for SLO riders. Learn more about all the ways to stay cool in the heat and all the best gear to keep you cool and comfortable. See you out there!
