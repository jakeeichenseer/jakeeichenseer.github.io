About Us

Eichenseer-House Woodfired Pizza was born in 2025 from a simple idea: great pizza starts with great people and a welcoming place to gather. Nestled in the heart of San Luis Obispo, our pizzeria was built to feel more like a home than a storefront, where the warmth of a crackling wood-fired oven and the scent of fresh dough set the tone the moment you step through the door. Inspired by old-world techniques and California’s love for fresh, local ingredients, every pizza we make tells a story of craft, care, and community.

Our dough is made fresh daily, slowly fermented to develop rich flavor and a perfectly airy, chewy bite. Each pizza is hand-stretched, topped with thoughtfully sourced ingredients, and fired in our traditional wood oven until the crust blisters to perfection. From classic favorites to seasonal creations inspired by Central Coast produce, our menu is designed to honor tradition while embracing creativity. At Eichenseer-House, pizza is more than a meal — it’s an experience.

Eichenseer-House Pizza is a gathering place for families, friends, students, and neighbors alike. Whether you’re celebrating a milestone, winding down after a beach day, or grabbing a casual weeknight dinner, we aim to make every visit feel special. Laughter, conversation, and the joy of sharing a great pie are at the center of everything we do. Our staff isn’t just here to serve you — we’re here to welcome you in.

Our slogan, “Our House is Your House,” isn’t just a phrase — it’s our promise. When you walk through our doors, you become part of the Eichenseer-House family. We invite you to pull up a chair, savor the moment, and make yourself at home.
